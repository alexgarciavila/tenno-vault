“These used to be museum fodder, ceremonial and all that golden piss talk. But now, real weapons. Void-touched, not fashion accessories, not conversation pieces. They are for killing. We good?”

—Cavalero

Incarnons are a weapon upgrade progression system that touches them to the Void. It increases their lethality through evolutionary upgrades and grants them an Alternate Fire that triggers Incarnon Form, a mode that further augments their power.

Some weapons are "natural" Incarnons, such as the weapons from the Zariman and from Sanctum Anatomica, which were originally ceremonial paraphernalia altered by the Void accident, and the experiments of one Albrecht Entrati respectively. Others require the installation of an Incarnon Genesis, which can be obtained through playing The Circuit in The Steel Path or purchased from Cavalero for platinum.

All weapon sub-types such as Prime, Wraith, and Vandal are eligible to install the Genesis for their respective weapon. However, Akimbo and Dual weapons are not considered the same weapon under the Incarnon system, and are not eligible for Incarnon Genesis installation.

Zariman Incarnon weapons are accessible by completing Angels of the Zariman. Sanctum Anatomica Incarnon weapons are accessible by completing Whispers in the Walls and The Deadlock Protocol. Incarnon Genesis items are obtainable by completing The Duviri Paradox and unlocking The Steel Path.

Acquisition

[edit | edit source]

Incarnon Weapons can be acquired by:

  • Completing Angels of the Zariman, and then purchasing the weapon blueprints with The Holdfasts standing from Cavalero in Chrysalith. Additionally, these blueprints require the player to be at least Mastery Rank 14, and are tradeable.
  • Completing Whispers in the Walls and The Deadlock Protocol, and then running Armatus, Deimos for a chance of the weapon blueprint and parts as part of a Rotation reward. Alternatively, collecting enough  Vessel Capillaries from Armatus will allow the player to buy the blueprints and parts from Loid in Sanctum Anatomica.
  • Completing The Hex, and then running Isleweaver on Duviri to collect enough  Scuttler Husk to purchase the  Thalys weapon blueprint from Acrithis or Dominus Thrax in the Dormizone.

Incarnon Geneses can be acquired by:

  • Completing The Duviri Paradox and unlocking The Steel Path, and then running The Circuit with the Steel Path modifier enabled to obtain up to two Incarnon Geneses every week. The rotation of Incarnon Geneses available changes with each week and can be seen in the table below. Genesis items can be installed on weapons by talking to Cavalero with the Duviri resources required.
  • Alternatively, the player may purchase the current week's available Genesis Adapters once from Cavalero's "Incarnon market" for 120 Platinum 120. This purchase also provides the resources used to install it.

Cavalero can also remove the Incarnon upgrade to return the Genesis Adapter, but the Duviri resources used for the installation are not refunded.

Mechanics

[edit | edit source]

By completing a number of challenges based on the weapon, Incarnon weapons can receive evolutions from Cavalero. Like other challenges (Riven mods, Nightwave, etc.), progress is tracked independently from mission progress. Each weapon has a total of 5 evolutions.

The first evolution unlocks the weapon's Incarnon Form, granting them an Alternate Fire mode or increasing their stats while changing their appearances. Activating the Incarnon Form requires certain conditions be met in missions, the method of which differs depending on the weapon.

For primary and secondary weapons (e.g.  Laetum,  Phenmor,  Felarx), the Incarnon Transmutation gauge that appears under the reticle must be charged by landing weakpoint hits.  Angstrum Incarnon Genesis and  Torid Incarnon Genesis are instead charged through direct hits. While active, the Incarnon Form uses its own separate ammunition, represented by the Transmutation gauge. Activating the Incarnon Form can be done at any level of charge, but the amount of Incarnon ammunition available will depend on how full the gauge was at the time the weapon transformed. Manually deactivating Incarnon Form by hitting Alt Fire again will deplete the entire gauge.

For melees (e.g.  Praedos,  Innodem), the Incarnon Form is activated by reaching a 6x Melee Combo (5x for Innodem) and then executing a Heavy Attack. Melee Incarnon Forms last for 180 seconds (90 seconds for Innodem and  Ruvox) and cannot be refreshed while active. This does not require consuming Melee Combo, so options such as Tennokai can be used to maintain the multiplier into Incarnon Form.

The remaining Evolutions provide 2 or 3 different selectable perks that further enhance the weapon's stats. Only one perk can be selected per Evolution, which can be changed freely with Cavalero or in the Arsenal.

Challenges

[edit | edit source]

Below are the challenges required to unlock the Evolutions of each natural Incarnon weapon currently available (Incarnon Genesis challenges appear in the next section). The first, fourth, and fifth challenges are identical between these Incarnon weapons, but the second and third Evolutions differ between weapons.

Progress for all challenges is maintained between runs, so the player does not have to complete them in a single mission.

  • Evolution I: Kill 100 enemies with the weapon
  • Evolution II:
    • Laetum/Phenmor/Felarx/Onos/Ruvox: Kill 8 Eximus with the weapon's Incarnon Form
    • Praedos/Innodem: Activate Incarnon Form 6 times in a mission
  • Evolution III:
    • Laetum/Phenmor/Felarx: Land 8/20/20 weakpoint hits on Void Angels with Primary fire without reloading
    • Praedos/Innodem: Complete 3 Heavy Attacks on Thrax Centurions or Legates
    • Onos: Get 5 weakpoint hits on Scathing and Mocking Whispers in Albrecht's Laboratories with Primary fire without reloading.
    • Ruvox: Inflict 3 Heavy Attacks on Scathing and Mocking Whispers in Albrecht's Laboratories
  • Evolution IV:
    • Zariman Ten Zero: Close 12 Ruptures in Void Flood
      • Player must have contributed to closing the rupture via depositing Vitoplast.
    • Albrecht's Laboratories: Open 20 Conduits in Disruption on Armatus, Deimos.
  • Evolution V: Complete a Solo mission with an Incarnon Weapon equipped in every slot
    • Any mission will suffice for this challenge.
    • Incarnon Genesis weapons are valid for the purposes of this challenge.

Incarnon Genesis

[edit | edit source]

“Incarnon Genesis

Hey.

Heard you been grubbing around in the Zariman's backyard. That little tinpot 'kingdom.'

Well, in my extensive downtime, I've been having thoughts about weapons. How I could upgrade 'em. Give 'em a sprinkle of Void magic. And guess what? The Void does what the Void does. Took my ideas and made them real. How about that.

So if you've picked up a... let's call it an Incarnon Genesis, why not? An Incarnon Genesis, bring it to me and I'll get it installed for ya.

Slaughter awaits.
Cavalero

PS. Yonta says I need to say this very clearly: I cannot adapt a weapon you do not own. I will need both the Incarnon Genesis AND the weapon it applies to. Understood? I am not running a charity here.”

Similarly to the Zariman weapons, the Genesis weapons have their own challenges, but only have 4 Evolutions in total. Installing the Genesis Adapter is considered the first challenge. Challenges only need to be cleared once per weapon type, for example clearing  Braton's challenges will automatically unlock perks for  Mk1-Braton,  Braton Prime, and  Braton Vandal when acquiring their Incarnon forms. The remaining challenges are as follows:

  • Evolution II: Complete a solo mission with the weapon
  • Evolution III:
    • Primary/Secondary: Kill 100 enemies with the weapon's Incarnon Form
    • Melee: Activate Incarnon Form 6 times in a mission
  • Evolution IV: Varies by weapon
