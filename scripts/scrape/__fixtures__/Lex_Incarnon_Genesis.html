Lex Incarnon Genesis is an item that is used to add Incarnon upgrades to the  Lex or  Lex Prime.

Acquisition

[edit | edit source]
  • Upon completion of The Duviri Paradox and Angels of the Zariman, and unlocking The Steel Path, players can earn Lex Incarnon Genesis from The Circuit's Steel Path to place on their Lex with the help of Cavalero in the Chrysalith. This Incarnon Genesis form grants it evolutions to a transformation and newfound strength.
  • Purchased from Cavalero for 120 Platinum 120, complete with the resources used in its installation. This purchase can only be made once.

Incarnon Genesis

[edit | edit source]
  • Installing the Lex Incarnon Genesis requires 20  Pathos Clamps, 70  Yao Shrubs, and 150  Saggen Pearls.
    • When installed, the first Evolution tier is unlocked. Each challenge additionally grants 5,000 affinity to the weapon, affected by Affinity Boosters.

Overview

[edit | edit source]
  • Incarnon Form is charged by filling the Incarnon Transmutation gauge that appears under the reticle by achieving weakpoint hits and then pressing the Alternate Fire button. 10 weakpoints hits will completely fill the gauge. A full gauge is not required to activate Incarnon Form.
    • Charges do not generate on the backpacks of most MOA variants, or vulnerability spots generated by  Sonar or  Detect Vulnerability in places other than actual weakpoints.
    • Weakpoints on corpses or Invulnerable targets do not build charges.
    • Individual Multishot bullets can build charges.
  • Mode switching is not instantaneous and has an animation equal to the weapon's reload speed.
  • Swapping to and from Incarnon Form will fully reload the normal form's magazine.
  • Instead of drawing ammunition from its reserves, the Lex's Incarnon Form uses a separate "magazine" with each charge producing 2 rounds to a maximum of 20. Once all ammunition is expended, the Lex reverts to its normal form.
    • Incarnon Form's magazine is not affected by mods.
    • Incarnon Form is not affected by Ammo Efficiency (such as  Energized Munitions).
    • Charges and Incarnon Form persists through all activities, such as holstering, bleedout, or being disarmed. However, charges and Incarnon Form are removed upon death.

Patch History

[edit | edit source]

Update 39.0 (2025-06-25)

  • Incarnon Adapters available in the current Steel Path Circuit rotation are now available for Platinum Purchase in Cavalero’s store in the Chrysalith.
    • Purchasing these Incarnons for Platinum will reward players with both the Adapter and the required resources to install it.

Update 38.6 (2025-05-21)

  • Fixed the Lex Incarnon Evolution Hoplite Virtue not increasing the Lex’s base damage.

Hotfix 38.0.8 (2025-02-06)

  • Fixed the Ready Retaliation perk not working on Lex Incarnon.

Hotfix 37.0.4 (2024-10-10)

  • Updated the following Incarnon Evolution descriptions to specify “Weakpoint Hits” instead of “Headshots”:
    • “X% Critical Chance on Weakpoint Hits”
    • “Weakpoint Hits charge Incarnon Transmutation; Alt Fire transmutes. Switching back will expend any remaining charge.”
    • “On Consecutive Weakpoint Hits.”

Hotfix 33.6.4 (2023-08-15)

  • Fixed the Lex Incarnon Genesis’ final evolution challenge (Get 5 Headshots with this weapon in Incarnon form in a single mission) considering headshots from all weapons.

Hotfix 33.0.11 (2023-05-24)

  • Fixed attachments not showing while riding a K-Drive (and secondary is equipped) on the Lex with Incarnon Geneses.

Update 33.0 (2023-04-26)

  • Introduced.
